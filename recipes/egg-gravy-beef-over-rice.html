Egg Gravy With Beef Over Rice

One of my favorite dishes to make. Silky smooth egg gravy with tender slices of beef smothered over rice.

Ingredients:

Serves 4

  • 500g beef flank/sirloin/topside
  • 2 large eggs beaten
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 stalk green onion finely chopped
  • Beef Marinate
    • 1 tsp cornstarch
    • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
    • 1 tsp cooking wine
    • 1 tsp sugar
  • Egg Gravy
    • 2 cloves garlic finely minced
    • 2 tbsp cooking wine
    • 300 ml chicken or beef stock
    • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Gravy Thickener
    • 2 tbsp cornstarch
    • 3 tbsp water

Recipe Steps:

  1. Step 1: Marinating the Beef

    2. Cut the beef into thin slices and add the marinade ingredients and let the beef marinate for 15 minutes.

  2. Step 2: Cooking the Beef

    3. Preheat 1 Tbsp of oil in a skillet. Add the beef slices and cook until they just start to turn color and start to lose the pink.

    Remove from the skillet. Don't overcook the beef.

  3. Step 3: Preparing the Gravy

    4. In the same skillet, add garlic and saute for 20 seconds until fragrant.

    Add the cooking wine to deglaze the pan. Scrape the bottom of the skillet to loosen the brown bits.

    Add the chicken/beef broth, oyster sauce, sugar. Bring to a simmer.

  4. Step 4: Thickening the Gravy

    1. Combine cornstarch with water. Pour this into the skillet and keep stirring until the gravy thickens.
    2. Drizzle in the beaten eggs and let them cook undisturbed for about 10 seconds and then gently stir it.
    3. Add the beef back in and stir to combine everything. Have a taste and adjust seasonings if needed. Add the chopped green onion and turn off the heat.

  5. Step 5: Serve

    6. Serve on top of warm with rice. Done!
