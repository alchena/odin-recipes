Egg-Gravy-Beef-Over-Rice Recipe

One of my favorite dishes to make, silky smooth egg gravy with tender slices of beef smothered over rice.

Ingredients:

Serves 4

  • 500g beef flank/sirloin/topside
  • 2 large eggs beaten
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 stalk green onion finely chopped
  • Beef Marinate
    • 1 tsp cornstarch
    • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
    • 1 tsp cooking wine
    • 1 tsp sugar
  • Egg Gravy
    • 2 cloves garlic finely minced
    • 2 tbsp cooking wine
    • 300 ml chicken or beef stock
    • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Gravy Thickener
    • 2 tbsp cornstarch
    • 3 tbsp water
